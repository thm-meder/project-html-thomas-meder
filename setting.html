Story

For the last two-hundred years, Seven Magus competed in a war to obtain the Holy Grail, who is said to grant any wish. To this end, these competitors summoned Heroes from the past to help them. As of today, four Holy Grail War were attended, but there never was a winner. Seventy years ago, the location of the war changed from a small town in Japan to a city in France, under the supervision of the Arclight family, a renown European family of Magus. Today, the fifth Holy Grail War will begin...

The City of Lisrick


The city of Lisrick os a fictive town located in South-Eastern France, and is under the protection of the Arclight Family. Due to the high-quality of its leylines, every 10 years, a new Holy Grail war is held for seven master chosen by the Mage Association or by the Church. The war is supervised by three entities: a representative of the Magic Association, another from the Church, and the Arclight family herself. Untill now, the wars were so violent that nobody ever managed to win the war. Will our valliant Magus succeed where every other failed?
